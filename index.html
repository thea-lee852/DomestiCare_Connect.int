About Domestic Connect

Domestic Connect is a comprehensive platform designed to simplify and streamline the process of hiring domestic helpers while ensuring compliance with all relevant legal and employment requirements. We aim to create a trusted and user-friendly environment for both employers and helpers to connect and build strong, professional relationships.

Our Mission

  • Empowering employers with the tools and resources needed to find qualified and trustworthy helpers.
  • Supporting domestic helpers in finding fair employment opportunities that align with their skills and preferences.
  • Promoting ethical hiring practices and fostering mutual respect in the domestic employment sector.

Safety and Trust at the Core

Trust was always at the core of Domestic Connect’s mission. To ensure both employers and helpers felt secure, every profile on the platform was verified. This verification process was designed to build a foundation of trust and transparency, so both parties could have confidence in their relationships.

The platform also offered easy communication tools to facilitate the interview process. Employers could message and video call potential helpers, enabling them to get to know each other before committing to a working relationship. This allowed both sides to ensure they were making the right choice for their homes and families.

Disclosure of Personal Data

Where disclosure of personal data is necessary to satisfy the purpose, or a directly-related purpose, for which the personal data was collected, we may provide such personal data to:

By submitting your personal data to us, you understand and consent to the transfer of such information as described above.

Retention of Personal Data

All personal data we collect will be stored only for a duration that is relevant to the purpose for which it is collected and for as long as permitted or required by the applicable law.

You have the right to request access to and correction of your personal data submitted to us. If you wish to do so, please send an email to: personaldata@dcc.com
